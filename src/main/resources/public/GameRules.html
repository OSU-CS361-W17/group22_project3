Battleship Rules

Object of Battleship: To become the winner of Battleship you must be able to find all five ships in your opponents fleet before they sink yours.

Setup Rules: you have five ships to place air craft carrier, battleship, submarine, Air Craft Carrier is five spaces long Battle ship is four spaces long Submarine is two spaces long you can not over lap the ships when placing them, they can be placed vertically and horizontally but not diagonal.

firing Rules: Each turn you fire one shot. then the enemy fires one shot. You can only fire at one spot once, cant overlap a shot. If shot hits a enemy ship the space will appear red to indicate hit. If miss becomes gray. The same goes for if your ships are hit. If enemy ship is destroyed it will be placed on the right side of the opponents board.

Scan Button: the scan button will check the space above, below, left and right of the last shot you made. it checks to see if there is an enemy in one of those spaces. If there is it will indicate that there is one nearby but it will not tell you which square.
